Pike Pila

Description

It is the cuisine of the Apatanii tribe, made with Aged/Fermented ingredients

Ingredients

  • Fermented Bamboo Shoots (hiiring)
  • Aged Smoked Pork (Huyii)
  • Liquidified special ashes (Pila) or alternatively solidifed special ashes (tappe)
  • Salt & Chilli according to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Pork until it is well cooked for consumption

  2. Add the Fermented Bamboo Shoots, in medium thin sliced pieces and cook until the shoots are well cooked Boil the bamboo shoots once separately and squeeze-rinse them for 2-3 times to remove the excess sourness

  3. Add Pila,Chilli to the the mix and cook it in very low flame until the signature Spicy , a tad bit Creamy umami taste arrives

  4. Enjoy it accompanied with preferably Rice as the main dish
