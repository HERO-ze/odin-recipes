Fried PRORK

Description

Pork friend with condiments for a simple, quick and tasty complement or as a snack

Ingredients

  • Chopped Pork
  • Garlic, Ginger , Onion , and Chilli
  • Oil for cooking and Salt for taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Pork until well cooked for consumption

  2. Cut two handfuls Onions into thin slices , Smash a mixed handful amount of Garlic cloves,Ginger bite-sized pieces in a piston

  3. Prepare the frying utensil with very small amount of Oil and heat

  4. Fry the Pork until it sizzles into a slight brown colour

  5. Add all the remaining sliced Onions,smashed Garlic-Ginger and the Chilli according to preference

  6. Fry until the Pork has a sizzled into a proper brown with some smoke coating

  7. Enjoy it as an accompaniment to a main dish such as rice or as a Quick tasty snack
